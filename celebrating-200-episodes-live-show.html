Celebrating 200 Episodes (Live Show)

A live milestone show recorded in front of an audience, with all three hosts in the room for the first time in three years. Marty opens with a state-of-play on 2021 versus 2024: cost of living, house prices by city, the jump in mortgage repayments, and rents. The three trade rate-cut predictions and Jason argues the RBA acts on delayed data. Jason then gives a long read on AI, from exponential growth and AI agents like Just Ask Xero to designing work around what AI cannot do and protecting human time and relationships. Nick roasts Jason's old share picks and the Mr Yum investment that got away, they cover Victorian state debt and the GST carve-up, then close on a reflective stretch about consistency, what the podcast has taught them, and the road to 300.
