EP 212

Will Trump Impact Australia?

Jason and Marty are joined by producer Tommy Jackett, back from seven weeks in the US filming a documentary on the second amendment and American gun culture. Tommy shares what he saw on the ground before the 2024 election, and the trio weigh US gun statistics against Australia's. They then turn to whether a second Trump term will affect Australia, covering corporate tax cuts, China tariffs, climate policy and US government debt near 36 trillion dollars. Marty and Jason close by comparing US and Australian debt, interest rates and inflation, and agree to revisit the numbers in a year.
