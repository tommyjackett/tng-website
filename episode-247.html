Melbourne Property Boom?

Why 2025 Could Spark a Gold Rush

Marty makes the bullish case that Melbourne could see a property gold rush over the next three or four years, led by boutique apartments and townhouses in the 600 to 750 thousand range. He points to migration, clearance rates back above 70 percent, a record 64 percent gap to Sydney and a 1 percent rental vacancy. Nick and Jason weigh what could go wrong, why investors are selling and owner-occupiers taking over, the hidden costs that push landlords out, and how first home buyers should act now with a broker and a buyer's agent.
