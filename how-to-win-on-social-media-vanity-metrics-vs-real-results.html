EP 250

How to Win on Social Media

Vanity Metrics vs Real Results

For episode 250 the trio get into social media for professional services and the gap between vanity and real results. Marty shares how a 400,000-view Instagram post brought zero clients while a small LinkedIn article brought two, and why the silent metrics of watch time and profile visits matter more than likes. They cover niching your platform, long-form strategy content, digital trust and balancing a personal brand. Nick explains his weekly email and why you need a system to measure what actually produces clients.
