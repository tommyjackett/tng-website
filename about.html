We're just getting started

It's finance, investment and business, but not as you know it. Each week Jason Robinson (CPA Accountant) and Nick Reilly (Planner) come together to bring you strategy and advice around the importance of knowing your numbers in life and business.

Numbers really do tell a story, so let them help you make it a good one. From ETFs and super to property strategy, side hustles and starting a business, The Numbers Game cuts through the noise with real talk, no jargon.
