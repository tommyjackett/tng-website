EP 194

Bucket Companies - Everything you need to know

Regular co-host Nick is away in Barcelona, so producer Tommy Jackett jumps in while Jason answers a listener's YouTube question about bucket companies. Jason explains what a bucket company is, why it pays either 25 or 30 cents in the dollar, and walks a sole trader example where splitting a 300k profit across a couple and a bucket company keeps an extra 36,500 in the family group. He covers getting money out with franking credits, the section 100A trap of parking income no one actually receives, using a bucket as an investment vehicle and for asset protection through a loaned-back PPSR security, why growth assets belong outside a bucket, and how to set one up properly rather than a bare ASIC registration.
