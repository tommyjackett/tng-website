EP 214

The Cost of Raising a Child

Nick returns from parental leave and the trio work through what it costs to raise a child in Australia. They cover the fall in fertility rates from around five children per woman in the 1950s to 1.66 today, childcare at up to 175 dollars a day, and lifetime schooling costs from 103 thousand through the government system to 307 thousand for private. Nick puts the all-in cost of a child at somewhere between 700 thousand and a million dollars. Marty and Jason weigh whether private school is worth it, and Nick makes the case for sitting down and planning the numbers before starting a family.
