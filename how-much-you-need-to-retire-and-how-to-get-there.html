EP 223

How Much You Need To Retire And How To Get There

Nick, Jason and Marty answer the question they get most as financial planners: how much do you need to retire and how to get there. They work an example from a target income of 150 thousand a year to the roughly 3 million in investments needed at a conservative 5 percent return, then cover tracking your net wealth every year and breaking a long goal into annual targets. The trio work through budgeting, using superannuation as the most tax effective vehicle, and how a clear plan builds financial wellbeing and calmer spending decisions. They close on balancing the numbers against what you value, from the FIRE movement to keeping the car you love.
