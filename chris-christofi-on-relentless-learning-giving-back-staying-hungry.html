EP 236

Chris Christofi on Relentless Learning, Giving Back & Staying Hungry

Jason and Nick interview Chris Christofi, founder and CEO of Reventon, who marks 20 years in business after starting in 2005 from his parents' living room with 348 thousand of debt. Chris traces a lifelong hunger to learn, from selling scratch tickets at age 10 in Cyprus to losing eight over-leveraged properties at 24 and building the Reventon formula. He walks through his property investment approach, why he now takes fewer but more strategic shots, and how a 20 year review reshaped his priorities. The conversation closes on philanthropy, the Vinnies CEO Sleep Out, and his view that there is no balance, only work life choices.
