EP 211

Understanding Financial Wellbeing

Nick leads a conversation on financial wellbeing, arguing it means being across your finances and comfortable with where you are, not how much money you have. The trio cover budgeting, emergency savings to absorb a shock, insurance as a fallback, and setting short, medium and long term goals. Nick maps three stages, financial turmoil, financial understanding and financial flourishing, and describes a six factor score that rates where you sit. They land on one starting point, know your numbers, and doing a budget to find where you can pull levers.
