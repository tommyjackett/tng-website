EP 195

Finding Personal Calm in Business Chaos

With Nick away, Jason and Marty are joined by producer Tommy Jackett to talk through managing anxiety and stress as a business owner. Marty opens on accepting that feeling anxious is part of being in business, then walks through his toolkit: strengthening self trust and trusting the people around you, foundational morning routines like Wim Hof breathing, normalizing stress into acceptance, and reframing criticism by asking what is good about this. Jason shares how the back end of the 2024 financial year broke his sleep and daily gym routine, and how meditation helped him reset. The trio cover breaking big goals into small daily wins, rolling 90 day team reviews, knowing when a path is not for you, and finishing with self care, useful resources, and putting your own oxygen mask on first.
