EP 225

Why You Need a Will

Jason, Nick and Marty make the case for having a will, after Marty admits he does not have one. They cover what happens when you die without a will, from state trustees controlling the estate to South Australia's default rule turning a 3 million dollar estate into 40 thousand dollars a sibling. The trio explain how to make a will, the roles of executor, beneficiary and guardian, the witnessing rules, and how a binding super or life insurance nomination can override a will. Several real family stories show the cost and conflict a missing or badly kept will can cause.
