EP 193

Clementine Boshevski - The Highs and Lows of Building Salt Lab

Jason and Marty host Clementine Boshevski, founder of topical magnesium brand Salt Lab, with Marty co-hosting while Nick is away. Clementine tells the full founder story: coming from a wellness gym she bootstrapped on credit cards, spotting a gap left by an untested imported product, and spending eighteen months in R and D before hitting the wall of manufacturer minimum order quantities. She explains the wellness science behind magnesium, why the first five years were all education, and how bringing marketing in house and going founder led made sales explode. The heart of the episode is resilience: an arson warehouse fire wiped out 1.5 million dollars of stock, and the community driven Build Us Back campaign carried the brand to its first million dollar month. She closes on cash flow in rapid growth, supplier terms, buying a farm, work and family boundaries, and expanding internationally.
