EP 206

Adam Diamond - Change Your Business, Change Your Life

Jason and Nick host business advisor Adam Diamond of Juno Advisory, with Marty away, to talk through what actually helps small and medium businesses grow. Adam explains why implementation beats advice, why he hires only senior people and brought on a CEO first, and how he frames a great hire as only an extra fifty thousand dollars. The conversation covers working on the business over working in it, meeting cadence and operational reporting, accountability, and why owners resist change. Nick shares the five metrics his team now uses to define success and align every decision.
