Has Realestate.com.au Become Too Powerful?

Nick asks whether realestate.com.au has become too powerful. With over 12 million monthly users, 1.7 billion in revenue and a net profit up 23 percent, REA Group dwarfs Domain, while vendors face thousands in marketing fees and agents cop steep subscription hikes. The trio get into the network effect behind the juggernaut, the postcode pricing the ACCC is now questioning, and whether CoStar buying Domain can bring real competition. They finish on REA as a share and the agents who sold theirs far too early.
