EP 233

How to Avoid Burnout and Still Grow Your Business

Marty opens with burnout data showing about 45 percent of business owners are burnt out and solo consultants far more. The trio work through why owners get stuck as the main revenue driver and how to move from consultant to business owner without cooking themselves. Nick makes the case for baby steps in growth, hiring admin before salespeople and knowing how long salespeople take to pay off. They close on deciding what you actually want, since a good income and the right clients can beat chasing size.
