Is It Time to Invest in Melbourne?

With Victoria battered by land tax and rental reforms and investors selling out one in four listings, Nick argues it is time to put Melbourne back on your radar. The trio compare four-year growth rates, Sydney and Perth up 24 to 37 percent while Melbourne has managed a measly 12 percent, and unpack why undersupply, high migration, the tipped biggest-city status by 2034 and stalled planning approvals set up a rebound. They run the numbers on a 700 thousand property, showing an interest rate cut and a rent rise can wipe out the land tax bill, and finish on Marty and Jason cautioning investors not to sell in a reactive FOMO phase but to understand the numbers and hold.
