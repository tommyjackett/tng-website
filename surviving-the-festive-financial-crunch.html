EP 215

Surviving the Festive Financial Crunch

Marty raises the festive cash flow squeeze facing small business owners and shares his own 2012 scare, when a growth pivot left him fearing he would close by January until an overdraft gave him breathing room. The trio walk through research showing many owners hold no cash reserves, have cut their own income, or are feeling personal stress, and why cash flow remains the top reason businesses fail. They cover proactive funding, seasonal planning, the building ATO balances after the September BAS, and short term overdrafts as a bridge over the holidays. Jason warns against going it alone with high interest funders, after a client took that path into liquidation.
