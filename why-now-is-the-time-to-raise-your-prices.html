EP 238

Why Now Is the Time to Raise Your Prices

Jason brings a pricing conversation to the end of the financial year and the trio work through hourly billing, fixed fees, retainers and value-based pricing, and where each one works or backfires. They cover a plumbing client whose 10 dollar rate rise added 240 thousand in revenue, the ethics of charging on a tax refund, and why a higher fee can signal expertise. Marty shares how he prices his own coaching, from a flat fee under 750 thousand of revenue to 6 percent above it, and Jason quantifies the profit a coach can add. The takeaway is to review your prices and costs each year so the numbers back what you are truly worth.
