How to Beat the Stock Market Without Losing Your Pants!

With markets on a high, Marty shares the investing lessons of the last five years, starting with the leveraged Macquarie play that cost him through the GFC. The trio open up on their own losses, then work through Buffett and Dalio principles, dollar-cost averaging, the 4 percent rule, concentrating on quality companies, stop-losses and drawdown frequency, and why rules and patience beat chasing quick wins.
