EP 240

How Money Beliefs Undermine Confidence and Lead to Self-Sabotage

Marty leads a personal conversation on how money and confidence beliefs formed in childhood quietly shape adult behaviour and self-sabotage. Jason opens up about being a teased chubby kid, Nick about growing up where money was always a topic, and Marty about an immigrant father who distrusted the wealthy. They cover survival versus abundance mindsets, building confidence through action, and identity in business and golf. Marty then coaches Jason live through a limiting belief about his marathon times, ending on a plan to work with a running coach.
