EP 216

From Dreams to Reality

Jason catches a sunrise segment on vision boards and asks Nick and Marty what they make of them after realising he has ticked off his old goals and feels stagnant. The trio compare vision boards with strategy boards, mind mapping and getting clear on goals, and debate why they seem to work through focus, the subconscious and manifestation. Marty warns against chasing goals from a place of lack and urges bringing the good feelings of the vision into today, while Nick argues for one or two near-term goals over far-off visions. Jason closes with the Jim Carrey ten million dollar cheque story and tips for building a board over the Christmas break.
