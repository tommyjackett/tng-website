EP 210

Pre-Approve or Lose!

Marty argues Melbourne buyers need a legitimate pre-approval to compete as spring stock rises and pre-approval activity climbs 40 percent year on year. The trio explain why going direct to one bank leaves borrowing power to luck, how different lenders treat living costs and stage three tax cuts, and what a pre-approval covers, an hour of discovery, a week to approval and a 90 day approval in principle. Nick urges buyers to stretch to their maximum and accept the discomfort, while the maths of upsizing in a down market favours the move. Jason closes with a warning to investors thinking of selling, speak to a broker and adviser before offloading and missing the upside.
