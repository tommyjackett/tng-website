EP 196

The Power of Early Retirement Planning

Nick leads a discussion on how the family home fits into a retirement plan and why to think about it early. The hosts cover the rising cost of holding an investment property with land tax, the tax free main residence exemption, and how you can lose it by claiming a home office or running an Airbnb. They explain treating the home as an asset you will eventually downsize, the roughly 1.3 trillion dollars of equity Australian retirees hold, guarantor loans and passing wealth to grandchildren, moving equity into super or shares, and the downsizer contribution that lets a couple put 600,000 dollars into super. They close on getting advice before selling a property near retirement.
