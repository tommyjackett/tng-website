EP 237

Good People, Bad Fit

How One Wrong Hire Can Cost You Millions

Marty, Jason and Nick work through why one wrong hire can quietly cost a business millions, opening on a business owner who admitted poor performance had cost him exactly that. They separate good people from bad culture fits, and cover setting high expectations from the first interview, reading lead indicators inside four to six weeks, and why holding onto underperformers drags down high performers. Marty shares the HR-backed methodology he uses to manage people out with a margin of care, plus the rising tide effect and setting a benchmark at three times industry average. Jason closes on the reminder that your high performers are watching how you handle your low performers.
