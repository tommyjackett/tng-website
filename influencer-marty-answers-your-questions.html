EP 184

“Influencer” Marty Answers Your Questions

With Jason away, Marty and Nick are joined by producer Tommy Jackett, who reads out the audience comments that flooded in after Marty's cost of living clip went viral on Instagram. They work through who people are blaming for the squeeze: the federal government and 14 interest rate rises, big corporations like Coles and Woolworths, household lifestyle and spending, and immigration. Nick then breaks down the Fair Work minimum wage rise and the growing gap between small and big business, with stats on rising insolvency and failure risk. Marty and Tommy add the human side, from a client who lost their business to a vegan restaurant that folded, and finish on how to think clearly about the numbers when a business is struggling.
