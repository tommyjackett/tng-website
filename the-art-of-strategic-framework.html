EP 209

The Art of Strategic Framework

Jason reports back from a three day business advisory course run by his old boss Brent Saleh and the Leaders in Business program, and the trio work through its five step strategic framework. They cover the small business survival statistics, why purpose comes first, and the steps from the ideal business through SWOT analysis, new strategies and a one page strategy document. The back half turns to leadership, covering delegation and burnout, the seven percent words model of communication, and reading each individual to get the best result. Jason also shares the gap between his firm Future Advisory as a compliance heavy accounting practice and the advisory business he wants to build.
