Nick Reilly

Nick Reilly is the planner who has played the game himself. Founder and CEO of Inovayt, the financial planning and mortgage broking group he started from scratch in 2007 and has grown into a 55-strong team settling more than $800 million a year, Nick spends his days helping Australians make their biggest money moves, and every Monday explaining them in plain English on The Numbers Game.

Money talk is personal for Nick. He grew up in a small town in regional NSW, raised by a single mum, where everything was earned and money was always a topic at the table. That is the lens he brings to every episode: build wealth deliberately, back yourself, and do not let the noise put you off. Real talk, no jargon, every week.
