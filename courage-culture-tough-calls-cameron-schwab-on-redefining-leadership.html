EP 249

Courage, Culture & Tough Calls

Cameron Schwab on Redefining Leadership

Cameron Schwab became the youngest CEO in AFL history at 24, running Richmond, Melbourne and Fremantle through some of their hardest years. He joins Jason and Nick to unpack leadership as carrying a weight forward, culture as an outcome of who you let in the room, and why authentic leaders have to be vulnerable. He shares the four questions every leader should answer, the four postures of a good conversation, and his daily trademark ritual of choosing courage over comfort, closing on making sense, meaning, progress and numbers.
