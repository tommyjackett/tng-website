2024 Review and 2025 Predictions

The trio review the predictions they made a year earlier and set out fresh calls for 2025. Jason grades his AI prediction a win, pointing to quiet integrations, an AI-generated profile photo and a ChatGPT vision demo, then predicts augmented reality, personal AI agents and wider blockchain understanding. Marty forecasts rate cuts, a Melbourne property surge of 14 to 15 percent and, long term, a robot Olympics by 2034. Nick tips a Liberal election win, a tough Australian economy and a share market lagging a Trump-fuelled United States, while Jason adds a call for GST reform.
