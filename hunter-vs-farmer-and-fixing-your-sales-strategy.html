EP 224

Hunter vs.

Farmer and Fixing Your Sales Strategy

Marty brings the hunter versus farmer sales framework, where a hunter chases and closes new deals and a farmer nurtures a client portfolio for referrals and organic growth. The trio place themselves as hunters by default, then work out why a business needs both, how the mix shifts by profession and career stage, and why hiring the wrong type for your sales model can hurt the business. They cover developing hunters into farmers, not burying hunters in admin, focusing on high return activities over raw call volume, and reframing the upsell as a transference of value. Jason maps it onto accountants, who tend to be farmers by default and win clients through proactive advice.
