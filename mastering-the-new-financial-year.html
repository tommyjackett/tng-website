EP 197

Mastering the New Financial Year

Jason, Nick and Marty run through a checklist of things business owners should master at the start of a new financial year. They cover running an annual planning day and sharing the vision with the whole team, building a monthly budget and reviewing it every month, and planning for seasonality with a ten month sales cycle. They move through reviewing your technology and where AI fits, an ATO mail automation example, using client NPS surveys and Google reviews as social proof, staying on top of compliance like payroll tax and single touch payroll, and reviewing business and asset finance terms with the bank each year.
