EP 185

Guidelines for Young Investors

Marty and Nick host, with Jason away and Tommy Jackett of VIDPOD in the seat, for a walk through the guidelines Marty used to teach his seven year old son Charlie to buy his first shares. Marty lays out the principles Charlie applied to evaluate a company, from whether it will last ten years to its competitive edge, rising revenue and profit, fair value and financial discipline. The conversation covers Warren Buffett's rule of not losing money, using ETFs and index funds to reduce risk, reading price to earnings and price to book, research tools like Jitta and SelfWealth, and the US housing shortage behind the chosen stock, Pulte Group. Nick and Tommy add why genuine interest and specialised knowledge matter more than the stock itself.
