EP 246

Joel Wermut - Reinventing Real Estate Tech With Prop App

Prop App co-founder Joel Wermut joins Jason and Nick to explain the app they call the Tinder for real estate, matching buyers and sellers with vetted agents through a localised tender. He walks through the rise of buyer's agents from 2 to 7 percent, the cost versus value of using one, the human check-in call behind the tech, and the bootstrapped startup journey. They close on runaway REA listing fees, with real examples of 4,500 and 15,000 dollar marketing bills, and why getting eyeballs on a property no longer needs to cost thousands.
