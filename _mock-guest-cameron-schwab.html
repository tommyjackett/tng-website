CameronSchwab

Cameron Schwab became the youngest CEO in AFL history at 24, and went on to run Richmond, Melbourne and Fremantle through some of the hardest years those clubs have known. Four decades of leadership at the deep end — hiring, firing, rebuilding and holding a room together when the results weren't there.

These days he coaches leaders through Design CEO, the practice he founded on a simple idea: leadership is a craft you can actually design, not a personality you're born with. He joined Jason and Nick to talk courage, culture and the tough calls that define whether a business — or a football club — survives them.
