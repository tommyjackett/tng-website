EP 208

Superannuation and the First Home Dilemma

Nick brings a coalition proposal to let first home buyers access their superannuation for a deposit, and the trio argue against it. They cover why property no longer guarantees a comfortable retirement, why tapping super could leave someone hundreds of thousands worse off, and the unfairness of investors already using super through an SMSF. Nick makes the case for a cleaner alternative: letting credit-worthy buyers borrow 100 percent of a property value so super keeps compounding while a home grows CGT free.
