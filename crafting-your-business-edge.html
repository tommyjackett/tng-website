EP 207

Crafting Your Business Edge

Marty opens with data showing rising business delinquencies and a sharp drop in average invoice values, then argues that point of difference decides who wins when the market tightens. The trio contrast differentiation with getting the basics right, drawing on mortgage broking, a Koala mattress callback, two Richmond cafes and referral-driven accounting growth. Nick credits his firm's edge to elite basics, ongoing education and trusting the right people, while Jason warns against cookie-cutter startups. They close on looking to other industries, scaling your edge and building a team that cares.
