EP 203

If I Could Start My Business Again Today...

Jason opens a round-table on what the three would do differently if they started a business again today. They cover writing at least a one-page business plan, setting up the accounting system and cash flow from day one, and investing in digital marketing and SEO far earlier. Nick and Marty share how Innovate grew on word of mouth and face-to-face value before the digital era, and why chasing every early client builds the pipeline. They close on finding mentors and peers early, with Marty recalling the keg lunches where six proven owners taught them business.
