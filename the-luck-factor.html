EP 188

The Luck Factor

Marty leads a discussion on luck, arguing it is far more controllable than people assume. Jason shares how he fell into accounting without ever choosing it and how a chance meeting sent him to a Bali conference that changed his path, while Nick separates life luck, being born in a safe country, from business results that are earned through effort. Marty lays out categories of controllable luck, young luck in how you conduct yourself, skill luck from being coachable and taking action, performance luck where the top twenty percent produce most of the results, team luck from A graders raising each other, and partnership luck from meeting complementary people. They land on the idea that behaviour, habits and the people around you create most of what looks like luck, and that life does not just happen to you, you can make it happen.
