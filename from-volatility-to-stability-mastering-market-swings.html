EP 204

From Volatility to Stability

Mastering Market Swings

Nick walks through asset allocation, splitting a portfolio into defensive and growth buckets and matching the mix to your age, stage of life and risk profile. He explains why investors sold in the COVID crash and locked in losses, and how a balanced profile lets you rebalance, take profit when growth runs and buy back in when markets fall. Marty shares his own 10 percent play-money rule and trimming Microsoft and Google, while Jason argues for a professional handling the quarterly rebalancing. The trio land on emotions being more volatile than the market and the value of a plan you trust.
