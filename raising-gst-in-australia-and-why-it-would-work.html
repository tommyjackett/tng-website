Raising GST in Australia and Why it Would Work

Jason makes his long-running case for raising the GST and cutting income tax to build a fairer, simpler tax system, after the idea lit up the YouTube comments. He compares Australia's 10 percent GST and 45 percent bracket at 190 thousand against the UK, Germany, France, Canada and the US, where higher VAT sits alongside much higher income thresholds. The trio also weigh taxing all property gains, protecting low earners through essentials and a 50 thousand tax-free threshold, keeping company profits onshore, and whether the change would stoke inflation. They finish on why no politician has had the nerve to try it.
