EP 235

Why Are Insurance Premiums Going Crazy in 2025?

Jason asks Nick why personal insurance premiums have skyrocketed in 2025. Nick walks through the four covers, life, total permanent disability, trauma and income protection, and explains how an industry review forced insurers to redesign income protection so older policies keep getting repriced until people leave. He shares real claims, a truck driver who lost a leg and a physio left with only thin default super cover, and shows how to keep cover affordable by extending the waiting period, reducing cover as assets grow and reviewing every twelve months. They finish on tax, income protection deductibility, an ATO data-matching warning, a super contribution hack and the Cbus claims delays.
